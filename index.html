My story

I have made my home base in Edmonton, Alberta for the past 15 years. Last year, my wife and I were blessed to each have babies and now we have three, amazing toddlers keeping us on our toes (in case you’re doing the math, this mama had twins)!
My journey into tech started with a diploma in Communications with a focus on Public Relations and Advertising. Over the past decade I have developed and honed my marketing skills within Edmonton’s real estate sector, during which time I outsourced much of the online technical work to consultants. Inspired by the minds and technical abilities of these consultants, I set out to learn how to code and develop my own technical skills!

With excitement (and, if I’m being honest, a little fear), I quit my day job in June, 2017 and started studies in the new, exciting world of tech! After building my first website, I’ve realized I found something that not only compliments my strengths & work-style but has given me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my career. I have since made a full switch to front-end developement, where I can use my organization, marketing skills and eye for detail to build websites that help build brands and grow businesses!
